Our Story

Where Luxury Meets Intention

LUXE was born from a simple belief: that beautifully crafted objects have the power to elevate everyday life. We curate only the finest products, working with artisans and brands who share our uncompromising commitment to quality, sustainability, and timeless design.

Founded in 2019, we've grown from a small Mumbai boutique to a trusted destination for over 50,000 discerning customers across India who believe what surrounds you shapes who you are.

Our Values

🌿

Sustainably Sourced

Every product is vetted for ethical sourcing and sustainable production. We believe luxury should never come at the cost of the planet.

🔍

Expertly Curated

Our buyers travel the world to discover exceptional products. Less than 5% of submissions make it onto our shelves — only the truly exceptional earn a place.

🤝

Artisan Partners

We partner directly with craftsmen and small-batch producers, ensuring fair wages and preserving traditional techniques that might otherwise be lost.
