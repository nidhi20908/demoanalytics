Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take? ▼
Standard delivery takes 5–7 business days. Express delivery is 2–3 business days, and Next Day Delivery arrives the following day by 8 PM. Delivery timelines begin once your order has been dispatched.
What is your return policy? ▼
We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, simply contact us within 30 days of delivery and we'll arrange a pickup and full refund.
Is my payment information secure? ▼
Absolutely. All payments are processed through 256-bit SSL encryption. We never store your card details on our servers and all transactions are fully secured.
Do you ship outside India? ▼
Currently we ship across all major cities in India. International shipping is coming soon — subscribe to our newsletter to be the first to know!
Can I track my order? ▼
Yes! Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a tracking link via email and SMS. You can also contact our support team at any time for order updates.
